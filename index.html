Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Threads & Trust

AR Fashion is a manufacturer and wholesaler of premium unstitched ladies' suits, rooted in the heart of India's textile tradition. Our journey began over a decade ago with a singular vision — to bring artisanal quality to the wholesale market.

We are not a marketplace. We are a manufacturer. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the mark of careful craftsmanship, whether born from the delicacy of hand embroidery or the precision of machine work.

  • Unstitched Ladies' Suits — Full Range
  • Handwork & Machine Work Specialisation

  • Wholesale & B2B Partnerships Only

Our Founder

A Lifetime Devoted
to the Loom

"Every suit we create is a promise — to the retailer who trusts us, and to the woman who wears it."

Mr. Kalra, the driving force behind AR Fashion, has spent more than a decade mastering the art and business of ladies' ethnic wear. His deep understanding of fabric, embroidery technique, and market needs has shaped AR Fashion into the trusted wholesale name it is today.

Under his stewardship, the brand has grown from a modest manufacturing unit into a respected B2B supplier — known for fair pricing, consistent quality, and personal commitment to every business partner.

His philosophy is simple: give the wholesaler something they can stand behind with pride. When a retailer's customer picks up a suit and loves it, that is AR Fashion's greatest achievement.
